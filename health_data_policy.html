Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-06. Effective: 2026-06-05.

This is a separate, conspicuous Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy for Fasting Tracker: IF Timer, published by Cloud Motion Lab LLC, in accordance with Washington's My Health My Data Act (HB 1155), Nevada SB 370, Connecticut SB 3, and similar state consumer-health-data laws. It is published in addition to, not in place of, our general Privacy Policy.

Plain-language summary. Fasting Tracker: IF Timer stores your health data on your device. We do not sell it. We do not use geofencing around health facilities. You can delete it at any time, and we will instruct our service providers to delete copies. We rely on your consent, which you give by entering data into the App.

Health Data We Collect

"Consumer health data" is defined broadly under the Washington My Health My Data Act to include any data linked or linkable to a consumer that identifies past, present, or future physical or mental health status, including inferred data, biometric data, reproductive or sexual health data, gender-affirming care information, sleep, nutrition, exercise, fasting, and weight. Fasting Tracker: IF Timer collects the following such categories from you:

  • Entries you provide in the App, such as fasting schedule, weight, water intake, mood, sleep window, or related metrics, depending on the features you use.
  • Device-level signals from Apple HealthKit only where you have given an explicit, app-specific HealthKit permission, and only for the categories you grant.

Why We Collect It

To operate the features you choose to use. To compute streaks, summaries, charts, and notifications you have asked for. To save your history locally so the App is useful across sessions.

How Long We Keep It

Until you delete it in the App, or until you delete the App from your device. We do not maintain server-side mirrors of your health data.

Who We Share It With

We share consumer health data only with the service providers listed below, and only as needed to operate the App. These providers are contractually bound to use the data only for the purpose we specify.

Authorization to Sell, Affirmative Statement

We do not sell your consumer health data. Under the Washington My Health My Data Act, an "authorization" to sell consumer health data is a separate document distinct from a general consent. Because we do not sell consumer health data, no such authorization is sought, given, or relied upon. If we ever change this practice, we will request a separate, opt-in authorization in advance, in the form prescribed by the Act.

Geofencing

Fasting Tracker: IF Timer does not implement any geofence around any in-person healthcare service, healthcare provider, or location offering reproductive, sexual, gender-affirming, mental health, or other healthcare services. We do not collect device location for health-related targeting.

Your Consumer Rights

  • Right to access the consumer health data we have, in a portable form.
  • Right to delete consumer health data we have. Deletion requests cascade to the service providers listed above.
  • Right to withdraw consent for collection or sharing at any time, with effect going forward.
  • Right to a list of all third parties with which we have shared your consumer health data.
  • Right to file a complaint with the Washington Attorney General. Washington residents also have a private right of action under the Washington Consumer Protection Act, RCW 19.86, which may carry treble damages.

To exercise any of these rights, email dev@slyapp.co with the words "Health Data Request" in the subject line. We respond within 45 days, with one 45-day extension where reasonably necessary, and we will identify any third-party service provider to which your deletion was forwarded.

Deletion Cascade to Subprocessors

When you delete consumer health data in Fasting Tracker: IF Timer, that deletion is local to your device. If any analytic event referencing health-related activity has been transmitted to TelemetryDeck, we will, on receipt of your deletion request, instruct TelemetryDeck to remove any data associated with the same anonymous identifier. If we ever maintain server-side health data, deletion will cascade to all subprocessors holding that data.

Children

Fasting Tracker: IF Timer is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect consumer health data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with health data, contact dev@slyapp.co and we will delete it within 30 days.

We may update this Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy. For material changes, we will provide at least 30 days' advance notice through an in-app banner. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
