Privacy Policy

Fasting Tracker: IF Timer by Cloud Motion Lab LLC
Last updated: April 14, 2026

Information We Collect

Fasting Tracker collects minimal anonymous analytics data through TelemetryDeck to improve the app experience. This data does not include any personally identifiable information. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

Health Data

Fasting Tracker may store weight entries and fasting history locally on your device using SwiftData. This health-related data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server. If you choose to connect Apple Health (HealthKit), data is shared only with Apple Health on your device per your explicit permission.

Analytics

We use TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics service, to collect anonymous usage signals such as which features are used and general app performance metrics. TelemetryDeck does not use cookies, does not track users across apps, and is fully GDPR compliant. No personal data is transmitted.

Subscriptions and Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives anonymous identifiers from Apple to track purchase state. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Storage

All fasting history, weight logs, water intake records, and user preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your data.

Third-Party Services

  • TelemetryDeck (anonymous analytics)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Apple StoreKit (in-app purchases)
  • Apple HealthKit (optional, user-initiated)

Children's Privacy

Fasting Tracker is not intended for children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect information from children. The app does not require an account and collects no personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
